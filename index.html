Welcome to CoinTrak

CoinTrak is the ultimate Cryptocurrency Companion app for iPhone and iPad. CoinTrak allows you to keep your portfolio in check with live price updates, conversion between any two currencies, a favoriting system, and even check balance and transactions of any BTC, LTC, or ETH address with the built-in blockchain explorer. Keep your Cryptocurrency Portfolio on track with CoinTrak!

Live Cryptocurrency Information

Cointrak currently employs the services of multiple data sources for its live, up to the minute price data. These sources include CoinMarketCap.com, CryptoCompare.com, and Blockcypher.com.

Make use of the Main Ticker page for price info for the top currencies ranked by market cap. Tap any currency for price and market information, and double tap for historical data.

Live Cryptocurrency Conversion

Convert between any of the top 100 cryptocurrencies or to USD through the intuitive conversion page. Select which currencies you want to convert from and to from the selector wheels, enter a value, and convert!

Track Specific Coins

Simply swipe left and tap on any ticker on the main ticker page to add it to favorites, where you can keep a closer eye on it without all of the other coins in the way. The Favorites Page allows you to store whatever coins that you wish and saves them when you close the app, and you can swipe left and tap to remove any coin from favorites as easily as it was added. The interface is identical to the main ticker page, and you can double tap to bring up the chart info just like in the main page.

Track your Investment

Check the balance of any Bitcoin, Ethereum, or Litecoin address and view recent transaction to and from yours, or anyone else's publicly available address with the easy to use Blockchain Explorer Page! Simply enter the desired address in the field, press the search button, and you can check the current balance and transaction history of an address. Use this function to make sure transactions have been registered on the blockchain before exchange.
